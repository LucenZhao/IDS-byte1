Who is the most influential men's tennis player during 2011-2015?

This is a simple but worth exploring question. Every year, the ATP players will earn points from each tournament they played, which will be accumulated and ordered as ATP ranking. From the rankings, we can approximately know who is the most influential player at this time.
However, how can we know who is the best player during a period of time, let's say, 5 years?

To answer this question, I explored the data of every ATP matches during 2011-2015. Because of the nature of ATP tournaments, players will be eliminated during the tournament, and only the best players can come to the finals. In order words, best players will tend to play more ganmes than others. Moreover, the best players in the world will attend most of the high-level ATP games, such as Grand Slam and Masters. Hence, by measuring the number of games a player played in these series, we can get a glimpse on their overall performance during this time.

Based on this simple assumption, I worked on following steps for visualization:

  • Filtering the data to keep only matches from Grand Slam and Masters
  • Construct the chart with directed links between winner and loser of each game
  • Setting a weight with the # from best_of (Grans Slam: BO5; Master: BO3. It is reasonable to do so since Grand Slams are more important)
  • Visualize the data and set an option for different surfaces

The results of visualization is shown below. In the graphs, the larger a node is, the more influential is the player.

  • All types of surface
  • Hard
  • Grass
  • Clay

From the graph, Djokovic is the most influential player, with imporssively most wins and almost no losses. Federer, Murray and Nadal are also quite influential during this period of time. The results are similar to people's impression on the "Big4" players during that time.

Djokovic has a great advantage in matches with hard surface. According to our results, he is the most influential player in hard surface matches with no doubt.

Although Federer is well-known for his dominance on grass surface, during 2011-2015, Djokovic and Murray has better performance in these matches.

Djokovic appears to be the most influential player with clay surface although Nadal is known to have a persistent strong performance on clay surface.

Outcomes
From the visualized charts above, we can say that Djokovic is the most influential player during this period of time, who showed strong dominance in all types of surfaces. Federer, Murray and Nadal are also shown to be one of the best players at that time.
